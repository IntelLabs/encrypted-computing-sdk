10  """
11  Encapsulates a `cstore` CInstruction.
12 
13  A `cstore` instruction pops the top word from the intermediate data buffer queue
14  and stores it in SPAD. To accomplish this in scheduling, a `cstore` should
15  be scheduled immediately after the `ifetch` for the bundle containing the matching
16  `xstore`.
17 
18  For more information, check the `cstore` Specification:
